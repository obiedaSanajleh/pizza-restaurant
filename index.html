About Us

Pizza Hut is a global restaurant chain known for its signature pan pizzas, pasta, and sides. Founded in 1958 in Wichita, Kansas, it has expanded worldwide with dine-in, takeaway, and delivery services. The brand offers a variety of pizza styles, including stuffed crust, thin crust, and hand-tossed, catering to different tastes. Its menu also includes wings, breadsticks, and desserts. Pizza Hut is recognized for its innovation, such as the introduction of the stuffed crust pizza, and frequently updates its offerings to keep up with market trends and customer preferences.
